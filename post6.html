Blue Cross of Idaho Intern

Overview:

Key Projects

Journey Maps

I joined in on a project that was intented to help workers at Blue Cross understand what the customer is going through as he/she proceeds through the process of getting accepted for healthcare.


I learned a lot about the design flow. I learned how companies use design thinking to help them get into the customers shoes. I learned how to work with others on a whiteboard to layout the customer experience.

Empathy Maps

Blue Cross did several different interviews and research to come up with five different customer templates, such as Settled Survivor or Convenience Conformer. They applied traits to these specific groups. I was tasked of designing rough drafts of empathy maps, which are used to understand how the user feels and really understand the customers perspective.

I talked with my mentor to create some Empathy Maps. I read a lot of articles on the design process for these maps. I learned how companies use them to market and design.

Competitor Analysis

Designers analyze other competitors to come up with pros and cons in order to inform their design. I studied the User Experience of three different health care websites, I picked functionality on their website to compare, and I came up with ideas on how to make the Blue Cross website better

I learned how to analyze design and compare. This is very helpful and can be used in many different cases. How do customers experience the Macbook vs the Microsoft PC ? What makes customer lean towards one side vs the other? I learned how to come up with those analysis and present the data.

UX Interviews

To get one on one with customer is the only surefire way to test your projects. I proposed the idea of doing some interviews of an audeince aged 21 - 25 to see how they feel about healthcare. In these interviews, I asked them not only about how they felt, but asked them to use the website to try to find a plan that fits them.

I learned how to write an interview, finding an audience, conducting the interview, and how to probe users for more information. I was able to gather some really good data from 10 people.

UX design wireframe

This is a work in progress. I am using the information I got from UX interviews to make wireframes. Wireframes are a prelimanary design for websites and apps.
